I'm Josh - a Graduate Engineer from UNSW, with a passion for Product Design.

Tallai Project Group

Building integrated system processes and technical-commercial alignment to streamline project delivery.

The primary projects I worked on are the upcoming Sydney Metro Line and the North Richmond Waste-Water Treatment Plant.

My role focused on optimising system operations and constructing frameworks for analysing projects, ultimately translating high-level client needs into detailed technical estimates.

CNFiTech

Digital Systems Electrical Engineering

CNFiTech is a electrical engineering contractor, with a focus on design, manufacture and install for switchboards.

My role was to collaborate closely with lead engineers to review, draft, and update single-line diagrams and electrical schematics using AutoCAD.
